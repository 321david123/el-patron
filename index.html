Nuestra Historia

A warm, family-style cantina in the heart of downtown.

Locals come to El Patron for generous plates of authentic Mexican cooking — street-style carne asada tacos, sizzling fajitas, and shareable parrilladas and molcajetes built for the table. Our full bar is famous for its towering margaritas, and weekends bring fresh pozole and menudo.

From a quick downtown lunch to a lively night out with friends, El Patron has been Rock Island's welcoming neighborhood favorite — the kind of place reviewers call "a very lively place with good food and a very friendly and welcoming staff."
